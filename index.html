      About Space4Kids
      Space4Kids is an educational project which in a spimple and engaging way brings astronomy and the mysteries of the Univers closer. We want to give children the opportunity to better explore space outside of school. We want to anwser children's questions, not dismiss them.  
      Ask us any questions!
